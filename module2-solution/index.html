Our Belief

The Word

We belief according to the scripture in John 1:1-5, that "in the beginning was the word, the word was with God and God himself is the word..." and this forms an important part oof our belief.
Any man that will come too God must believe in the power of the word which is Christ Jesus.

Prayer

We belief in prayer as a communication tool with the almighty. We believe according to the scriptures that whenever we call on Him, He hears us.
This has mould us and established our feets in faith that whatever we ask in the name ofn Jesus will be done by our Father in heaven.

Worship

God dwells in an atmosphere of worship, the 24 elders recorded in the book of revelation worship Him day and night with the song of halleluiah.
Our belief in worship is to eenhance His presence and to express our unending love to Him. Man was created for worship.
